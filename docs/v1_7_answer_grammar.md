# v1.7 Answer Grammar

**Introduced:** `v1.7.0-alpha`  
**Purpose:** define how the core question may be answered without overclaiming.

## Core question

> Can a final trinary witness distinguish earned-one from raw expression pressure, latent overcrown, relation debt, return debt, and false-one pressure under controlled synthetic-field adversarial weather?

## Answer states

| state | meaning | required evidence |
|---|---|---|
| `+1` | yes, answered inside controlled synthetic-field adversarial weather | all required lanes visible, baseline enemies beaten, fresh/holdout pressure survives, role-dependence bounded, final false-one crowns remain zero |
| `0` | partial / bounded / unresolved | at least one lane, baseline, holdout, or role-dependence condition remains unresolved |
| `-1` | no; the full claim is not earned | false crowns, lane collapse, dead-safe equivalence, baseline equivalence, or role-label recounting defeats the claim |

## What `+1` would not mean

A `+1` closeout would not mean role-blind discovery, unknown-field generalization, physical proof, cosmology proof, or observed-universe bridge.

## What `0` protects

`0` is not embarrassment. It is the witness state that keeps partial evidence from becoming a false crown.

## What `-1` protects

`-1` protects the repo from saving a broken claim by poetic wording. If the final witness cannot distinguish the required lanes under controlled synthetic-field adversarial weather, the roadmap must demote or redesign.

Native witness remains:

```text
C_Z = min(D, P, R, B)
```
