# Review Discipline

ZeroGateSim uses a DREED-like witness posture for serious claims.

The purpose is not to judge truth automatically.

The purpose is to show pressure before action.

## Operating separation

Every result should separate:

- **Evidence** — what the simulation produced.
- **Logic** — what follows inside the model.
- **Inference** — what may be suggested.
- **Metaphor** — how the idea is made speakable.
- **Speculation** — what remains unproven.

## Main review questions

Mechanism-boundary:

What did the model actually measure?

Integration-modularity:

Where does this result belong: engine, report, theory note, future work, or hold?

Witness-translation:

What claim survives when bounded for an outside reader?

Trace:

Can the reader follow the path from signal to gate to report?

Repair-minimality:

What is the smallest useful repair?

Overdo risk:

How could this become fog, ego, bureaucracy, fake certainty, or simulation theater?

## Rule

No toy simulation result becomes cosmic proof.

No beautiful language outruns the measured output.

No graph gets to wear a crown because it looks profound.

The primate is allowed to survive. It is not allowed to falsify the witness.
