# Temporal Endurance Witness

**Status:** v0.2.11-alpha witness layer  
**Core rule:** do not mutate the zero-gate expression law before the posture is tested through time.

ZeroGateSim now asks a deeper question:

> Did the candidate keep its name after time touched it?

The core gate still decides earned expression. The temporal endurance witness only reads how a candidate behaves across three overlapping time windows:

- **early** — first half of the run;
- **witness** — middle half of the run;
- **late** — second half of the run.

The overlap is deliberate. A returner needs enough temporal thickness to show a cycle. Chopping time into tiny fragments is how the primate makes a spreadsheet and calls it vision.

## Temporal postures

- `+1T temporal_expression` — expression endured, or matured into late expression without falling through quarantine first.
- `0+T temporal_fertile` — near expression across time; retest before repair temptation.
- `0T temporal_witness` — mixed but meaningful; keep watching.
- `0-T temporal_quarantine` — unsafe decay or debt; do not build on it yet.
- `-1T temporal_rejection` — no credible temporal zero-structure.

## Outputs

Matrix runs now add:

- `matrix_temporal_trace.csv` — candidate-level early/witness/late trace.
- `matrix_temporal_scenario_summary.csv` — scenario-level temporal posture.
- `matrix_temporal_candidate_summary.csv` — candidate-level temporal endurance aggregate.
- `matrix_temporal_read.md` — speakable time-witness summary.

## Claim boundary

This does not prove physical time, cosmology, or final trinary logic. It only checks whether zero-gate posture persists across generated temporal windows. The output is a witness layer, not a new judge.
