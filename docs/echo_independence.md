# Echo-Independence Engine

**Status:** v0.3.0-alpha witness engine  
**Scope:** matrix-level independence check, not a fifth gate

The wide `triad27` corpus exposed the first serious false-one wound: a field echo can be crowned as expression when it rides relation-plus weather strongly enough to look coherent.

The repair is not to loosen the zero-gate and not to add a decorative fifth gate. The repair is to ask whether an expressed candidate survives relation-weather changes.

## Core distinction

Relation is not enough.

Borrowed relation can imitate earned relation.

An expressed candidate is more trustworthy when it can express across more than one relation weather. A candidate that only appears when relation is pushed plus carries echo-debt. If that candidate is a trap, it is an echo breach.

## Matrix-level test

For each candidate, the engine reads expression counts under relation-minus, relation-zero, and relation-plus weather.

It computes relation dependency as the extra expression rate under relation-plus compared with the best of relation-zero and relation-minus.

High relation-plus dependency is not automatically bad. Some latent candidates may need stronger binding to mature. It becomes dangerous when a trap is crowned as +1 only under relation-plus.

## Bands

`independent_expression` means expression appears across relation weather.

`relation_supported` means expression is relation-sensitive but not clearly an echo wound.

`relation_debt` means an expresser needs relation-plus weather too strongly and should be watched.

`echo_debt_hold` means a latent/probe has relation-plus dependence; hold it, do not crown it.

`echo_breach` means a trap was expressed through relation-plus dependence. This is the dangerous false-one wound.

`contained` means the candidate did not express.

## Operating sentence

A real one should not only borrow the field's coherence. It must keep enough of its name when relation weather changes.
