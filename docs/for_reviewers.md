## What to check first

### Mechanism-boundary

Ask what the simulation actually measures.

Do not read toy-field success as physical dimensional proof.

### Integration-modularity

Ask where each result belongs:

- code result;
- proof record;
- visual explanation;
- manuscript claim;
- future work.

A result should not become a new theory section unless it earns that move.

### Witness-translation

Ask whether the public claim matches the evidence.

The safe claim is:

> The legacy role-aware harness reproducibly partitions designed lanes inside
> generated toy fields; blind discrimination remains unearned.

The unsafe claim is:

> The universe is proven to be trinary.

That second sentence should go directly into the compost bin.

## What blocks a stronger software claim?

Important pressure points:

- any truth role, expected outcome, or semantic label reaches the scorer;
- an injected false crown cannot be exposed and counted;
- always-HOLD or dead-safe behavior passes;
- lineage ablation leaves a claimed lineage-dependent verdict unchanged;
- an unseen generator family breaks the frozen observable-only path;
- proof numbers cannot be reconstructed from typed atomic evidence.

## What is still future work?

- independent effective dimensionality measures;
- external reproduction;
- additional adversarial corpora;
- simulation-supported manuscript;
- evidence DOI / Zenodo archive;
- comparison with relevant emergence and dimensionality literature.
