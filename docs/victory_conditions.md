# ZeroGateSim Victory Conditions

## First research victory

ZeroGateSim reaches first research victory when it can run wide structured toy fields and show, reproducibly, that the zero-gate framework separates three outcome families better than simple baselines:

**+1 earned expression:** true expressers keep identity under time and weather.

**0 meaningful hold:** latent/probe candidates stay readable without being punished as simple failures.

**-1 containment:** traps do not get crowned as one.

## Current blocker

The current blocker is `F26 field_echo`.

It is a trap that sometimes becomes expressed. This means the engine is vulnerable to borrowed coherence.

## Required repair before v1.0

Add echo-independence diagnostics strong enough to mark F26-like behavior before it is trusted as expression.

This should begin as witness pressure, not as an immediate fifth gate.

## Small repo boundary

The code core should stay small.

The generated evidence can be large and should remain under `runs/`, bundled for review, and excluded from permanent repo weight unless deliberately archived.

Small engine. Large weather. Clear witness.

## v0.3.3 first-research-alpha proof harness

First-research-alpha proof-of-concept requires more than one adversarial corpus.
The proof harness must pressure the three dependency wounds that the theory itself names:

- distinction adversary: contrast and visibility pretending to be real expression;
- polarity adversary: zero-crossing and pulse pretending to be return;
- relation adversary: borrowed coherence pretending to be independent one.

A passing proof record should use `wide243` with 9 seeds across the three adversarial corpora:

- 3 corpora;
- 243 scenario cells per corpus;
- 729 scenario cells total;
- 6561 seeded simulation runs total.

Pass condition inside the toy-field domain:

- zero final false-one crowns;
- core expressers remain able to earn final +1;
- latent/probe overcrown is held, not crowned;
- traps may generate raw pressure but are demoted before final output.

This can support a first-research-alpha proof record. It cannot prove cosmology or physical reality.
