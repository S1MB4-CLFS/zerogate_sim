# DREED Preflight for Post-Test Manuscript

## Mechanism-boundary

Does the paper clearly distinguish:

- raw local expression;
- earned-one final expression;
- latent overcrown;
- false-one demotion;
- toy-field behavior;
- physical/cosmological claims not made?

## Integration-modularity

Does every result have a landing place?

- theory section;
- simulation method;
- result table;
- limitation;
- future work;
- appendix.

If a result does not have a landing place, HOLD.

## Witness-translation

Can a reader understand the paper without knowing the private development history?

The public paper must not require the reader to believe in the author, the assistant, or the metaphysical origin story.

The machine and claim boundary must carry the weight.

## Trace

Every variable must be defined near first use.

Every figure must have a caption.

Every proof-number must point to the proof record.

## Example-fit

Do not use poetic examples as if they are simulation evidence.

## Repair-minimality

If a section overreaches, narrow the claim before expanding the paper.

## Overdo risk

The main overdo risk is turning a toy-field proof-of-concept into implied physics.

Do not do this.
