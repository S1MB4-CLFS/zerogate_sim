# Manuscript Plan

## Title

Zero-Gate Dimensional Genesis: A Simulation-Supported Foundation for Earned-One Witness

## Abstract spine

This paper presents a speculative generative framework and toy-field simulation study of zero-gate dimensional emergence. The original framework proposed that candidate freedoms become expressible only through distinction, polarity, relation, and return. ZeroGateSim operationalized this proposal and exposed a necessary repair: raw local expression is not final earned expression. A final trinary witness is required to distinguish earned-one, structured zero-hold, and false-one demotion. Across original and fresh-seed adversarial proof records, the earned-one witness demoted 2,388 raw false-one pressures with zero final false-one crowns inside generated toy fields. The result supports the internal coherence and testability of the framework, not claims of established physics or cosmology.
