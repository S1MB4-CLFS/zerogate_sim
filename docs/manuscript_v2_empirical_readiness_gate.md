## Why writing waits

Public availability, a release tag, a clean repository, or a locally green
firewall does not make a scientific manuscript ready. Manuscript v2 must follow
the empirical result; it must not help choose, rationalize, or cosmetically
upgrade that result.

`v1.8.0-alpha` establishes infrastructure only. It deliberately contains no
scientific scorer threshold and opens no holdout labels.

## Required evidence before positive manuscript prose

All of the following must be executable and preserved in artifacts:

1. exact observable-only schema and forbidden-field firewall;
2. predictions frozen and hashed before any held-out label join;
3. expected freeze-receipt and split-manifest hashes recorded outside the join;
4. label-permutation, identifier-renaming, row-order, and duplicate controls;
5. evaluator failure capability, including visible injected false crowns;
6. non-constant behavior and failure of always-HOLD/CROWN/RESIST baselines;
7. transparent lineage-bearing predictor path with a claim-relevant ablation;
8. scientific thresholds, abstention rules, and decision grammar fixed before holdout;
9. unseen generator-family separation with cross-split duplicate blocking;
10. one-pass frozen holdout evaluation with uncertainty and exact denominators;
11. complete provenance for scorer code/config, data contract, predictions, joins, and labels;
12. artifact-generated v1.8.4 closeout decision.

## Decision routing

| v1.8.4 result | manuscript action |
|---|---|
| `SUPPORTED_BOUNDED` | positive manuscript v2 planning may begin, bounded to the exact evidence |
| `HOLD` | no manuscript; identify the missing evidence and repair locally |
| `INVALID_EVIDENCE` | no manuscript; invalidate the run and repair the authority boundary |
| `FALSIFIED` | no positive v2 claim; a negative/correction paper requires separate authorization |

`SUPPORTED_BOUNDED` is necessary, not sufficient, for publication. A later
writing checkpoint must still inspect claim language, limitations, licensing,
authorship, citations, figures, and archive/release actions.

## Cohesion rule

Do not draft the conclusion first. When the gate opens, manuscript structure
must be generated from the exact versioned evidence map:

```text
historical theory lineage
-> v1.7.11 correction
-> v1.8 observable firewall
-> lineage-bearing predictor and preregistration
-> baselines and falsifiers
-> unseen-family holdout
-> empirical closeout
-> bounded claim and limitations
```

No approximation, anticipated result, or friendly external sharing may stand
in for that sequence.
